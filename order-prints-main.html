Order Prints     

Step 1 Pick a package and decide which photos you want to print.

Step 2  Find your package listed below, click it to be directed to ordering document.

Step 3 Fill out document with contact info, address, and select photos.

Reminder  For wallet sizes, you can only use one photo for each of the 4 prints. For other sizes
like 4"6"+ you can select different photos for each print, if your package comes with multiples.

Step 4 After you submit the form, I will review it
and email you a confirmation with paypal invoice within 24 hours.

Step 5 After payment is received, the order will be made
and shipped within 48 hours. Once it has shipped, I will send you confirmation and a tracking number.


If you have any questions or seek assistance, please feel free to email autumngailphotography@gmail.com
