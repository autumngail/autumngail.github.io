  Autumn Gail : Amateur Photographer

I have been interested in photography from
a young age. I am a junior in college, majoring
in Graphic Design. Last year I purchased my first
camera and then I built this website from scratch.
My interests include painting, web design, piano,
and scifi-tv shows. I love enhancing my skills in
all forms of visual art. Photography has helped me
look at my world in a more artistic way, with a
new kind of eye. My most favorite thing to take
photos of is sunsets, or the sky in general.
