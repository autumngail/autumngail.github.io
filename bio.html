  Autumn Gail : Amateur Photographer

        My name is Autumn Gail Dildine. I am a college
student located in Randolph County, AR. My major
is Graphic Design and my interests include painting,
web design, rock music, piano, and scifi TV shows.
Last year I purchased my first camera and I built
this website! I am so excited to pursue photography,
as I'm very interested in enhancing my skills and
experience in all forms of visual art.

        My aspiration as an amateur photographer is to
capture the beauty of my subjects and share it with
the world. As a service, being able to offer my
clients their precious moments, frozen in time,
makes me proud of my work and motivates me to
keep seeking more opportunities.

        Thank you so much for visiting my website!
If you have any questions or ideas please visit my
contact page or feel free to send me an email
to autumngailphotography@gmail.com
